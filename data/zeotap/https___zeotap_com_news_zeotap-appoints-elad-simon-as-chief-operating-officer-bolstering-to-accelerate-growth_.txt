Zeotap Appoints Elad Simon as Chief Operating Officer, Bolstering Operational Excellence to Accelerate Growth in the CDP Market
Berlin, 5th November, 2024 – Zeotap, a leading customer data platform (CDP) company, is pleased to announce the appointment of Elad Simon as Chief Operating Officer (COO). Elad, a recognised industry leader with an impressive track record in scaling companies and driving operational excellence, brings valuable expertise to Zeotap’s leadership team. As Zeotap embarks on the next phase of its ambitious growth journey, Elad’s strategic vision and deep understanding of operational frameworks will play a pivotal role in expanding Zeotap’s market presence and further strengthening its position as a CDP leader.“On behalf of the entire Zeotap Board, I am excited to officially welcome Elad Simon as our new COO! Elad’s proven track record will be a tremendous asset to Zeotap as we continue on our ambitious growth journey”, commented Danie Heer, CEO of  Zeotap. “We are confident that his leadership skills and experience will help us strengthen our position as a leader in the CDP space. Welcome, Elad – we’re thrilled to have you on board!”
Elad brings a wealth of experience in the MarTech, AdTech and SaaS space, working in companies such as Google and Taboola, as well as leading his own SaaS startup (Craft.io). Elad’s knowledge aligns well with Zeotap’s vision to revolutionise customer data management and drive impactful results for clients worldwide.
In his new role, Elad will lead the company’s operational strategy, with a focus on optimising processes, supporting rapid expansion, and ensuring customer success at scale.
