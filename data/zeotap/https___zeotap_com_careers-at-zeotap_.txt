Our vision
At Zeotap, we envision a future in which businesses can deliver personalised and data-driven customer experiences while putting customer privacy at the front and centre. We are dedicated to helping brands monetise their customer data and easily attain commercial outcomes. To achieve this, we have developed and offer must-have software and data solutions to support our customers in reaching their desired business outcomes across marketing, sales, and customer service. We differentiate ourselves by providing addressiblity in customer data. This easy-to-use platform drives faster results with data privacy and compliance focus, supported by best-in-class customer support.
Our values
Our values define who we are and how we work daily with customers, prospects, and team members. We let them come through in all that we do!
Pioneer Dare to be differentWe are not afraid to take risks and think outside the box. We are always looking for new and better ways to do things. We want to push boundaries and deliver value through innovation and creativity.
Adaptive
Change is always an opportunityWe remain flexible and responsive to changing circumstances. We are open to new ideas and approaches, as well as listen to and understand the perspectives of others. We are willing to learn and grow from new experiences.
Challenger
We think, act, and win above our weight classWe take on challenges that others may consider impossible to solve. A strong sense of ownership and a desire to succeed drive us. We put in the hard work and effort necessary to achieve our goals.
Trustworthy
Actions speak louder than wordsWe consistently act with integrity and accountability and focus on value creation for our colleagues and customers. We take pride in doing the right thing, even when it may be difficult. We stay true to our word and deliver on commitments.
