Zeotap CDP features
Customer 360° view
Create a comprehensive view of all your customer data that links online and offline identifiers and attributes for improved personalisation, predictive analytics, and cross-department customer alignment.
LEARN MORE
Predictive audiences
Machine-learned segments give you audience insights that help better predict customers’ likely behaviour to prevent churn, increase CLV, and improve cross-sell and upsell campaigns.
LEARN MORE
Omnichannel journey orchestration
Delivering personalised customer journeys allows you to deliver ‘Next Best Action’ and ‘Next Best Offer’ at scale, increasing your ability to reduce churn and attrition, as well as more effectively upsell and cross-sell.
LEARN MORE
Consent orchestration
Our DIY consent control centre allows you to set up consent and marketing preferences rules to reflect customer choices across all your martech and adtech channels for improved customer experience and complete GDPR compliance.
LEARN MORE
Privacy and GDPR
We are built in Europe for complete European compliance. Our customer data platform is easily extensible and designed to meet all privacy frameworks, including CCPA and GDPR.
LEARN MORE
Cookieless solution Zeotap ID+
Continue to identify and address your customers in a cookieless ecosystem with the only customer data platform with a universal ID linked to every customer profile to future-proof your advertising revenue.

Turnkey integration solutions
