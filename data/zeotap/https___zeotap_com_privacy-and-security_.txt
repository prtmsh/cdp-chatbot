Privacy and Security: We Protect Your Customer Data
Zeotap Products are designed to meet the European compliance standards and beyond
Protecting your data: our commitment and approach
At Zeotap, we acknowledge that the security and privacy of information and data created, maintained, and hosted by us are critical to the success of our business and the trust of our partners. We commit to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our customers’ data safeguarded at all times. Our privacy and security remain the cornerstone of our CDP. It dictates that we apply enterprise-grade security measures over every inch of our product. We continuously implement best practices, identify and mitigate risk as a matter of priority, and constantly evolve our security measures to protect our customers’ data.

Security and Privacy above all
Built for EuropeWhile most CDPs are made for North America, Zeotap has been conceived in Germany and developed for the stringent European regulation. Our platform is compliance-first by nature.
Certified complianceZeotap is continually re-certified for multiple international privacy and security certifications, including ISO 27001, ISO 27701, ISO27017, ISO27018, SOC2 Type2, CSA Star and GDPR Seal.
Designed around consentAll of our solutions place user consent and preferences at the cornerstone, helping ensure proper governance and complete compliance at all time.
Our privacy certifications
We believe that even the toughest data privacy regulations still leave plenty of room for managing and activating customer data, as long as it’s done right. And that’s why we engineer to meet the highest data privacy standards in the world.

Our security certifications
Our security measures exceed industry standards for data protection and securityWhen it comes to handling customer data, security is paramount. Zeotap has over ten years of experience in ensuring that every step of the data journey is held to the highest standards.
ISO/IEC 27001Our platform has coherent and comprehensive information security controls in place that have resulted in certification for security management for over five years running.
ISO/IEC 27701 ISO 27701 is a standard that provides guidance on the protection of privacy, including the management of personal information and demonstrates compliance with regulation around the world.
ISO/IEC 27017:2015 Our controls-based use of cloud services has been certified by the ISO/IEC 27017: 2015. This certification promotes a safer cloud environment.
ISO 27018ISO/IEC 27018 ensures the protection of personally identifiable information (PII) in public clouds acting as PII processors.
SOC2
Type2SOC2 certifies that we've following the right practices and controls to safeguard the privacy and security of customer data stored in the cloud.
CSA Security, Trust, Assurance and RiskAwarded the CSA STAR certification for 4 years running based on successful ISO 27001
certification and additional cloud-based requirements.
HIPAAThe certification ensures that our Customer Data Solutions adhere to the strict standards required for managing Protected Health Information (PHI), offering a dependable solution for healthcare organisations.
TISAXTISAX is a European automotive industry-standard information security assessment (ISA) catalog based on key aspects of information security such as data protection and connection to third parties.
