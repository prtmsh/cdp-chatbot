Google Cloud Partnership
A premier partnership to unlock limitless opportunities
What does our partnership entail?
The partnership between Zeotap and Google Cloud provides businesses with data-driven solutions enabling customers to access, manage, and activate customer data through Zeotap CDP while leveraging Google Cloud’s robust infrastructure and analytics capabilities.
Benefits for customers
As a Premier Partner, our seamless integration into the Google Cloud Platform and Google Marketing Platform stack maximises the potential of customer data to provide valuable insights, optimise marketing strategies, and drive growth.Enjoy access to exclusive beta tests to stay ahead of emerging trends and other key benefits:
Easy setupThanks to one-click deployment and simple configuration, Zeotap CDP offers lower latency, and reduced data transfer costs on Google Cloud, including BigQuery and VertexAI.
Easy-to-use UIZeotap CDP is designed for hands-on marketers to easily build and activate segments or orchestrate real-time journeys. No coding or technical skills are required.
Wide range of out of the box integrationsWith over 150 pre-built turn-key integrations, you can effortlessly connect Zeotap CDP with a range of the most utilised marketing technologies in just a few clicks.
Native Custom solutionsAs a partner built 100% on Google Cloud, Zeotap CDP offers native connectivity between data sources on Google Cloud and Google Ads, automating the full flow of data.
Google Marketplace Zeotap CDP is available on Google Marketplace and you can take full advantage of the benefits such as a streamlined procurement process, commit recognition and funding opportunities.
Instant ValueZeotap CDP can be hosted in your Google Cloud instance ensuring that you maximise the value of your commitments and take advantage of available credits.
