How can predictive customer analytics impact your business growth?
Predictive analytics can help you gain a deep understanding of your customers, resulting in more effective and highly targeted marketing campaigns. Understand your customersCustomer analytics and predictive insights give visibility into diverse data sources. Transforming them into actionable insights through customer profiling helps marketers better understand customer needs, preferences, and behaviours, improving targeting.Create targeted marketing campaignsDeep predictive customer insights allow marketing teams to identify and deliver more relevant and tailored omnichannel buying experiences across channels based on their profile and interaction history, which improves engagement.  Achieve higher conversionsFeatures like predictive audiences and journey orchestration enable customer targeting and empower marketers to create more personalised and effective campaigns. USE CASE 1
Customer profiling
Deliver personalised experiences at scale across channels. Zeotap CDP enables customer profiling by consolidating IDs and attributes, like purchase history or behavioural data from various sources, into centralised customer profiles. Marketers can use these unified, real-time CDP insights into each customer for tailored content recommendations. It allows tracking customer behaviour and dynamically selecting the most optimal content for each user, leading to more effective marketing strategies and higher conversion rates.

USE CASE 2
Identify channel preferences
Understand each customer’s preferred channel. With Zeotap CDP, marketers can analyse a customer’s interaction history for an omnichannel approach. Zeotap CDP allows customer segmentation by channel engagement history, such as insight into which audience interacts more via email than social media. It enables customer targeting with tailored messaging by channel, leading to improved campaign performance, higher engagement and response rates, and a seamless and unified brand experience, regardless of the channel they use.

USE CASE 3
Retain high-value customers
Target your most valuable customers. Zeotap CDP helps marketers identify these customers. Data attributes like purchase history are analysed to segment qualifying profiles, enabling a targeted list of customers who meet the defined high-value criteria. Custom programs, including special offers, rewards, recognition and referrals, can then be created and tailored for these selected customers to encourage continued loyalty. The result is increased customer retention and advocacy from customers who provide maximum lifetime business.
