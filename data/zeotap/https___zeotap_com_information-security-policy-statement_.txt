Information Security Policy Statement
At Zeotap, we are committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, availability and privacy of our information assets as well as the information assets of our customers. We ensure that Zeotap’s products and services comply with leading industry practices as well as regulatory, operational and contractual requirements. We continuously strive to enhance our security processes and deliver products and services to customers under all contingencies. We pride ourselves on: strictly adhering to our information security policy and privacy policy;rolling-out information security updates timeously;providing employee awareness training;adding additional security measures through information security innovations;safeguarding customer’s information; andconduct periodic security assessments on our information systems. If you have found a bug, please contact us using the below email address. [email protected] Thank you!
