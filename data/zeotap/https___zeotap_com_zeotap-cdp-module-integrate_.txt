KEY FEATURE
Sources
UnifySay goodbye to data silos and easily connect your data sources to Zeotap CDP for a unified view of your customers. Our ingestion process, error validations, and alerts ensure accurate data insights.
SecureZeotap CDP is built around the principles of privacy-centric data collection. Our secure and reliable platform collects and manages customer data while maintaining the highest levels of privacy and security.
SimpleOur customer data platform simplifies the process of reverse extracting, transforming, and loading (ETL) data from warehouses. With Zeotap CDP, managing data is easy and seamless.
KEY FEATURE
Destinations
ControlWith our customer data platform, you are in complete control of data transfer. Easily manage what and when data is sent to marketing and advertising platforms in real-time and batch.
Future-proofZeotap CDP is future-proofing your data management needs. We are the only customer data platform to activate audiences using CRM data - from identifiers like email addresses and telephone numbers - in Google DV360.
TechnologyOur customer data platform simplifies data integration with advanced technologies like Webhooks, Pub-Sub, and plug and play integrations. Easily manage and connect data sources with our trusted platform.
KEY FEATURE

InsightsAccess real-time data through our powerful APIs when and where you need it with our customer data platform. Zeotap CDP offers Audience Membership and Calculated Attributes capabilities for advanced insights.
FuelWith our customer data platform, you can power your applications with unified profile data. Access valuable insights and improve customer experiences to drive business growth.
SpeedWith our high-performance and fast response time, Zeotap's CDP is benchmarked twice as fast as the competition. We excel in volume and speed and handle bulk profiles with ease.
