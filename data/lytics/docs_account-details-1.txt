Suggest EditsThe following configuration options are available within the account settings Account Details section.
Account Name
Label for your account, which shows up in the account switcher, should you have access to more than one Lytics account.
Domain
Primary domain associated with your account.
Contact
Email address of the primary account contact.
Allow Access via API
To share profile data, such as audience membership, with integrations such as Google Analytics, profiles must be accessible via our personalization API.
Account ID (Non-configurable)
Your unique Lytics account identifier. Your primary identifier used in all API calls.
Account Number (Non-configurable)
Your unique Lytics account number. Used primarily for quick reference to which account you are currently within.Updated about 1 year ago Table of Contents
